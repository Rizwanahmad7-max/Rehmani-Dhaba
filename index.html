Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Dough banao milk powder, maida aur thoda doodh mila kar.
  2. Goliyan banao aur halki aanch par golden brown hone tak fry karo.
  3. Unhe garam sugar syrup me daal kar 1 ghanta soak hone do.

Barfi

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Ghee

Recipe

  1. Milk aur sugar ko thicken hone tak cook karo.
  2. Ghee aur dry fruits daal kar mix karo.
  3. Thoda set hone do aur squares me cut karo.

Motichoor Ladoo

Ingredients

  • Besan
  • Sugar
  • Ghee

Recipe

  1. Besan ka batter banao aur boondi fry karo.
  2. Boondi ko syrup me daal kar mix karo.
  3. Gol ladoo bana lo aur thanda hone do.
